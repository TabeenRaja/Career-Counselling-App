About PathFinder

Guiding professionals toward fulfilling careers for over a decade

Our Story

Founded in 2012, CareerPath Counseling began with a mission to help individuals discover their true career potential. Our founder, Dr. Emily Rodriguez, recognized the growing need for personalized career guidance in an increasingly complex job market.

Today, we've helped over 5,000 professionals navigate career transitions, find their dream jobs, and build fulfilling professional lives. Our evidence-based approach combines psychological assessments, industry insights, and personalized coaching to deliver exceptional results.
